NetSuite for Zoho Expense

A two-way integration that allows you to sync your expense categories and users in Zoho Expense and import your expenses from Zoho Expense to NetSuite and logging it as a vendor invoice once paid.

Procurement Cycle Solution

A customized solution that helps in improving the procurement cycle of a business. It includes a flexible approval routing process for Purchase Orders, vendor bill, Batch Payment, and E-way match. This bundle also provides an easier way of selecting vendors for bills from one or more Purchase Orders and creating Bill Payments from different bills or expense on time within NetSuite. It also automates the generation of payment and increases the tie-in to the corresponding SFTP folder from the bank.

SoBoNet Integration

A customized integration that can handle credit card processing seamlessly with invoice entry for seamless financial management and enhanced efficiency.
